# Amputee Unmet-Need Mining -- Summary Report

## Executive Summary

This analysis examined **58** amputee-related YouTube channels across **741** videos, collecting **6,503** comments.  Of these, **1,252** were classified as relevant to prosthetic-user experience, and **276** contained an explicit wish or improvement desire.  The sections below present category rankings, co-occurrence patterns, and a comparison between upper- and lower-limb populations.
