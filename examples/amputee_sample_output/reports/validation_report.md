## Methodology Notes

### Academic Sources

- **turner_mcgregor_2020**: Socket fit as primary factor affecting rehabilitation
- **manz_2022**: Lower limb systematic review (N=8149)
- **cordella_2016**: Upper limb prosthesis requirements
- **salminger_2023**: Upper limb focus groups
- **henao_2025**: Upper limb barriers

### Limitations
- YouTube comments represent a self-selected, English-skewing sample.
- Comment coding was performed via keyword matching and may miss nuanced or indirect references.
- Academic benchmarks were selected from a targeted (not exhaustive) literature review.
- Co-occurrence of categories in a single comment does not imply causation.
